About Novarex

Novarex leverages Kadena's Chainweb, a Layer 1 PoW blockchain with 20 parallel chains, delivering unparalleled scalability and security for Africa's financial ecosystem.

With Pact's human-readable smart contracts and Kadena's gas stations, we empower developers to create cost-effective dApps that drive financial inclusion and economic growth across Africa.

Our solutions are tailored for African markets, addressing challenges like high transaction costs and limited access to financial services with innovative blockchain technology.

Novarex Ecosystem

💳

Novarex Wallet

A secure, user-friendly wallet built on Kadena, enabling seamless transactions and financial services for African users.

  • Instant P2P Payments - Send and receive KDA with zero latency
  • Token Swaps - Convert KDA to stablecoins and other assets
  • Verified P2P Market - Secure trading with KYC-verified users
  • Merchant Solutions - QR-based payments and POS integration
  • Financial Access - Pay bills, buy airtime, and send remittances
  • Multi-Signature Security - Enhanced protection for user funds
🌐

Novarex Social

A vibrant social media hub on Kadena, fostering community engagement and rewarding participation across Africa.

  • Social Network - Post, share, and earn KDA rewards
  • Community Channels - Region-based groups for live chats
  • Builder Spaces - Developer collaboration hubs
  • Expert Connect - Collaborate with Kadena experts
  • Project Showcase - Share hackathon ideas and projects
  • Decentralized Identity - Secure, user-controlled profiles
🌱

Novarex AgriFi

Empowering African farmers with blockchain-based financing, turning agriculture into a sustainable income stream for KDA holders.

  • Stake-to-Earn Pools - Stake KDA to fund farm cycles
  • Real Yield - Farmers repay in produce revenue
  • Cross-Chain Bridge - KDA ↔ IST liquidity flows
  • Partnership with Infinite Sprouts
  • Sustainable farming investment opportunities
  • Transparent Supply Chain - Track produce from farm to market
